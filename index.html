Who am I?

As a graduate of the University of Oklahoma, my time there has been filled with many experiences that I value even to this day. These experiences have allowed me to learn vital skills over the past six years of my life and education. Some of the top things I still hold close include the ability and willingness to figure things out, understanding when to seek help, critical thinking, and improving communication. I believe these are all important traits that lead to growth and continuous improvement. I hope to follow through with my convictions and surround myself with like-minded individuals who push each other to learn new things and work efficiently.

Initially planning to pursue dentistry, I always had a lingering feeling in my gut about that path. I knew I wouldn't be able to continue leading my life toward a future I saw no fulfillment in. After graduating from college, rather than pursuing a route aligned with my bachelor's degree in Public Health, I ultimately decided to shift my attention to what I truly enjoyed: technology. I enrolled in a software engineering bootcamp.

I find passion in figuring out solutions and creating innovative technologies for organizations. This idea of being able to create something out of nothing amazed me. I hope to be able to create impactful applications that addresses real-world issues, and then eventually specialize in developing efficient software solutions for modern day healthcare scenarios. With my background in healthcare and public health, I believe this allows me to be more insightful when it comes to clients (patients, doctors, etc.), allowing me to understand the context of their situations and craft solutions tailored towards those needs.


My resume reflects a diverse career trajectory with a specialized focus in healthcare, now enhanced by coding expertise gained through the Springboard Software Engineering Bootcamp. During the bootcamp, I mastered both frontend (HTML/CSS, JavaScript ES6+, ReactJS, jQuery, Axios) and backend (Node.js, Express, Python, Flask, SQL, PostgreSQL, RESTful APIs) technologies, as well as testing with Jasmine. I hope to apply my knowledge to contribute to the success of your company and continue to learn and grow alongside the team!
